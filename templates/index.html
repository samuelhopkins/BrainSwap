Frequently asked questions

How does this work

Simply specify in your profile subjects you would feel comfortable assisting other students with as well as subjects you need assistance with. Using this information along with your major(s) we can recommend students for you to contact. You are also able to search for students to assist you or for you to assist based on the aforementioned profile information.

Is this service anonymous

Mostly. The only explicitly public information is your user name and summary. If you decide to contact another user they will receive your email as means of contacting you back. Information like profile classes can be inferred from matches, but it is never actually disclosed.

Are there rules about charging money for assistance?

As stated in the about section, though BrainSwap is not necessarily a marketplace for paid tutors, we do not discourage users from establishing professional tutor relationships or otherwise.
